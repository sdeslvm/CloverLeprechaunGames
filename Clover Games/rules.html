Game Rules

Main Game

  • General Information: The player controls a character that catches falling items and gold coins.
  • Falling Items:
    • Gold Coins: Collecting a gold coin gives the player +1 to their balance.
    • Other Items: Collecting any other item (not a coin) results in a -1 loss from their balance.
  • Golden Clover: Collecting a golden clover triggers a bonus game.

Bonus Game

  • Start of Bonus Game: After collecting a golden clover, a beautiful screen appears, informing the player about the start of the bonus game.
  • Duration: The bonus game lasts 10 seconds.
  • Game Mechanics:
    • Various colored clovers start appearing on the screen.
    • The player must only click on the golden clovers:
      • For each golden clover collected: the player receives +1 coin.
      • If the player clicks on a clover of another color: the game ends, and the player loses.

Quiz

  • General Information: The quiz consists of 30 questions on the theme of clovers and luck.
  • Question Structure:
    • Each question has 4 answer options.
    • Questions are presented to the player in a random order.
  • Final Screen: At the end of the quiz, the number of correct answers is shown. The player can return to the main menu.
